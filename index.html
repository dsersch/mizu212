MIZU 212 is finally back!

Thank you for your patience for the past 2 years.

Mizu has been a staple Shabu Shabu restaurant on Sawtelle for over 30 years. We pride ourselves in serving organic and ethically grown vegetables as well as humanely raised meats.

Shabu Shabu is one of the most enjoyed styles of hotpot in Japan, where the family can come together and enjoy conversation while partaking from the same pot. Our broth is flavored with combo ( or kelp ) to give the broth a rich umami flavor without taking anything away from the high quality meat we offer.
