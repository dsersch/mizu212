About Mizu212

Mizu has been a staple Shabu Shabu restaurant on Sawtelle for over 20 years. We pride ourselves in serving organic and ethically grown vegetables as well as humanely raised meats. Along with our premium meats, we also offer the highest grade of Japanese A5 Wagyu.

Shabu Shabu is one of the most enjoyed style of hotpot in Japan, where the family can come together and enjoy conversation while partaking from the same pot. At Mizu, you can also enjoy the traditional Japanese style at our modern bar seating, where each person uses their own pot. A great selection of Sake is also available to pair with our Shabu Shabu. Our broth is flavored with Konbu (or kelp) to give the broth a rich (mild) umami flavor without taking anything away from the high quality meat we offer. Shabu Shabu is the healthiest way to cook/ boil but don't forget to ask us about our special dipping sauces!
